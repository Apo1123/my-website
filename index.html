How It Works

Our patch works in three easy steps to give you peace of mind when eating out or trying new foods.

Step 1: Apply the Patch

Simply stick the patch on your skin, and it stays secure while you go about your day.

Step 2: Connect to the App

The patch connects seamlessly to the app, which tracks allergens in real time.

Step 3: Receive Alerts

Get instant alerts on your phone if an allergen is detected, helping you avoid potentially harmful foods.

Key Features

  • Real-time allergen detection: Instant alerts sent directly to your phone.
  • Easy to use: Simple application and seamless app integration.
  • Comfortable and discreet: Lightweight and designed for all-day wear.
  • Long battery life: Keeps working for up to 72 hours without needing a charge.
